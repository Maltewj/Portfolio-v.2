Installation concept

One of the more interesting projects i have worked on is this installation concept. We had a collaboration with Fjord & Bælt from Kerteminde on Fyn, which is a tourist attraction and research institution. We had to create a concept of an installation which could be used in the exhibition.

We worked in teams of four people and my team and i developed the idea of a research and science room, where visitors and more specifically children could pretend to be scientists working in Fjord & Bælt. We made different activities in the room, such as a quiz, VR game.
